{% trans "How does it work?" %}

  • Citizens sign up as Heritage Monitoring Scouts, and can begin monitoring Historic Cemeteries and Historic Structures right away!
  • To monitor Archaeological Sites, Scouts must first choose a region of interest, and then wait until an administrator assigns a site to them.
  • Once an Archaeological Site has been assigned to a Scout, that Scout can view the site's location, and log a Scout Visit!

{% trans "How do I log a visit?" %}

  1. Print out a Mission Sheet
  2. Check the owership of the site! This is very important.
